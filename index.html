About

I am a second-year CS PhD student at Cornell University, advised by Sainyam Galhotra. My research sits at the intersection of data management and large language models, building LLM agents that discover, integrate, and reason over structured data — more efficiently, reliably, and autonomously.

Before Cornell, I completed an M.Eng. in Systems Engineering at Cornell University and a B.Sc. in Statistics at the University of British Columbia.
